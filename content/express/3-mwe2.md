The Modeling Workflow Engine 2 (MWE2) is a [declarative, externally configurable generator engine](https://www.eclipse.org/Xtext/documentation/306_mwe2.html). To enable DDK to load the correct fragments, we have to edit the .mwe2 file to ensure the necessary plugins are loaded into the runtime.
